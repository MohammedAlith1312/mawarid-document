# My Request — Approved

The **My Request - Approved** page shows all your document requests that have been fully **approved** by all required approvers.

## Functions

- View all your approved requests
- Download the final signed document with digital signatures and stamps embedded

## What Happens on Approval

- Each approver's **digital signature** and **stamp** are automatically placed on the document at the configured position.
- The signed document is stored and available for download.

## Notes

- Approved requests are read-only.
- The signed document can be downloaded or shared from this page.
