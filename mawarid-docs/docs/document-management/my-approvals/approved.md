# My Approvals — Approved

The **My Approvals - Approved** page shows all requests that you have previously **approved**.

## Functions

- View a history of all requests you have approved
- Access the signed document (with your digital signature embedded)

## Notes

- These are read-only records of your completed approval actions.
- Your digital signature and stamp have been embedded in each document at the configured position.
