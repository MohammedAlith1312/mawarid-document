# My Approvals — Pending

The **My Approvals - Pending** page shows all document requests that are currently **awaiting your approval action**.

## Functions

- View all requests assigned to you for approval
- Review the document and requester details

## Actions Available

| Action | Description |
|---|---|
| **Approve** | Digitally sign and approve the document — your signature/stamp is placed automatically |
| **Change Request** | Return the document to the requester with comments for changes |
| **Reject** | Reject the document with a reason |

## Notes

- Once you **Approve**, your digital signature and stamp are automatically embedded at your configured position on the document.
- Once you **Change Request** or **Reject**, the requester is notified.
