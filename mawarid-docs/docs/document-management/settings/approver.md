# Approver

The **Approver** settings page allows approvers to upload their **digital signature** and **stamp** which will be used when they approve documents.

## Functions

- Upload your personal digital **signature** image
- Upload your official **stamp** image
- Preview how the signature/stamp will appear on documents

## How It Works

When an approver approves a request, the system automatically:
1. Retrieves the approver's uploaded signature and stamp
2. Places them on the document at the pre-configured position
3. Saves the signed version as the final document

## Notes

- Signatures and stamps must be uploaded as **image files** (PNG recommended with transparent background).
- Each approver manages their own signature and stamp from this page.
- Only the approver themselves can update their own signature/stamp.
