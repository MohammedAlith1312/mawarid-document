# Requester Flow

The **Requester** role requires **no login**. Requesters interact with Cafe4X entirely via a QR code placed at their seat (cabin).

## How to Place an Order

1. **Scan the QR Code** — Use any mobile device camera or QR scanner app to scan the QR code placed at your seat/cabin.
2. **Browse Items** — The system automatically identifies your location and seat. A list of available items is displayed.
3. **Select Items** — Choose the items you want and specify quantities.
4. **Place Order** — Submit the order. The order is immediately sent to the provider assigned to your location.
5. **Wait for Delivery** — The provider prepares and delivers your order to your seat.

## Notes

- No account or login is required.
- The QR code uniquely identifies the **location** and **seat/cabin** — orders are automatically routed to the correct provider.
- Items shown are based on the active product catalogue managed by the Admin.
- QR codes are generated and managed by the Admin from the [Location Seats](./admin/location-seats) page.
