# Employee

The **Employee** master page manages the employees who are assigned to maintenance tasks.

## Functions

- View and manage employee records available for task assignment
- Assign employees to sites and teams

## Notes

- Employees are assigned to work order tasks in the [Work Order Task](../work/work-order-task) page.
- Assigned tasks appear in the employee's [My Task](../my-task/pending) view.
